    Hello! I am a 5th year Ph.D. student in Electrical and Computer Engineering at University of Southern California, supervised by Shrikanth (Shri) Narayanan. My research interests are in Speech Processing, including ASR and Speaker Diarization, as well as Multimodal AI.

    Previously, I graduated Summa Cum Laude from UC San Diego with a B.S. in Electrical Engineering . I was born and raised in Nagoya, Japan until I graduated from High School. My Japanese name is 徐安峰 / Yasumine Jo.
